About Dhamaka:

"Dhamaka" is a 2021 Indian Hindi-language thriller film written and directed by Ram Madhvani. Starring Kartik Aaryan as the lead, the movie is an official remake of the 2013 film "The Terror Live." The plot follows Arjun Pathak, a former top news anchor now hosting a radio program, who receives a threat after interviewing a terrorist responsible for a major explosion in Mumbai. Arjun seizes the opportunity for a comeback by negotiating with the terrorist to exclusively broadcast their phone conversations in real-time. The story unfolds as the nation watches, revealing the chaos and exploitation within the newsroom, the police, broadcasters, and the government.

As the live broadcast continues, Arjun faces personal revelations, including the true identity of the terrorist and the tragic consequences of the bridge collapse, including the loss of his ex-wife Saumya. The movie takes a dark turn as Arjun, devastated by the false accusations and the loss of his loved ones, decides to end his life by detonating the explosive in his building.
