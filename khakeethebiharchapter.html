About Khakee The Bihar Chapter:

"Khakee: The Bihar Chapter" is an Indian crime thriller streaming television series created and written by Neeraj Pandey. Produced by Shital Bhatia under Friday Storytellers, the series features a star-studded cast, including Karan Tacker, Avinash Tiwary, Abhimanyu Singh, Jatin Sarna, Ravi Kishan, Ashutosh Rana, Nikita Dutta, and more. Premiered on Netflix on November 25, 2022, the series unfolds in Sheikhpura, Nalanda, and Patna districts. It revolves around the life of IPS officer Amit Lodha, portrayed by Karan Tacker, as he tackles the notorious Ashok Mahto gang's criminal activities while dealing with his personal challenges.

The plot delves into the real-life character, Pintu Mahato, who becomes Chandan Mahato in the series, a member of the Ashok Mahto gang that has been active in Nalanda and Sheikhpura for years. SP Amit Lodha, played by Karan Tacker, strives to bring justice to the region by dismantling the gang. The series explores the intricate balance between crime and personal life, creating a gripping narrative set against the backdrop of Bihar's law enforcement challenges.
