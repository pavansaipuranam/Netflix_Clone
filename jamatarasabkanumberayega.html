About Jamatara - Sabka Number Ayega:

"Jamtara – Sabka Number Ayega" is an engaging Indian crime drama that delves into the world of social engineering operations in the Jamtara district of Jharkhand. The series, created and directed by Soumendra Padhi and written by Trishant Srivastava, made its debut on Netflix in January 2020, with a second season premiering in September 2022. The narrative centers around a group of small-town youth running a successful phishing racket. Their operations take an intriguing turn when they encounter a corrupt politician eager to stake a claim in their illicit business. The plot thickens with the arrival of a newly appointed police superintendent determined to take on the entire network of cybercriminals.

The cast, featuring talented actors such as Amit Sial, Dibyendu Bhattacharya, Aksha Pardasany, and others, brings the characters to life, adding depth to the gripping storyline. With themes of crime, corruption, and the clash between law enforcement and cybercriminals, "Jamtara – Sabka Number Ayega" offers a riveting portrayal of the complex dynamics at play in the underbelly of cybercrime in a small Indian town.
