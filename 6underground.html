About 6 UnderGround

"6 Underground" is a 2019 American vigilante action thriller film directed by Michael Bay. The story follows American billionaire Magnet S. Johnson, who fakes his death to assemble a covert vigilante squad known as the ghosts. The team, each assigned a numbered nickname, aims to take down criminals and terrorists that the world fails to pursue. The film stars Ryan Reynolds, Mélanie Laurent, Manuel Garcia-Rulfo, Adria Arjona, Corey Hawkins, Ben Hardy, and Dave Franco. The ghosts embark on a mission to stage a coup d'état against the ruthless dictator of the fictional Central Asian nation of Turgistan.

In the plot, the ghosts, led by One, carry out missions with specific roles assigned to each member. After a successful coup in Turgistan, they part ways until their next mission. The film explores themes of justice, personal philosophy, and the consequences of their actions. The story unfolds with high-stakes action sequences, political intrigue, and personal dilemmas within the vigilante team. The movie concludes with a glimpse into the personal lives of the characters after their mission, highlighting the impact of their choices on their futures.
