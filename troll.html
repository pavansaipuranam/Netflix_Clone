About Troll

The 2022 Norwegian epic monster film, "Troll," directed by Roar Uthaug, unfolds a thrilling narrative as a diverse group of scientists, led by paleontologist Nora Tidemann (played by Ine Marie Wilmann), discovers the awakening of an ancient troll in the mountains of Dovre. As they investigate the mysterious occurrences and attempt to halt the creature's destructive rampage, the film explores the intersection of Norwegian folklore, science, and government intrigue. The storyline intensifies when conventional weapons prove ineffective against the massive troll, leading Nora to delve into mythology for a solution.

Released on December 1, 2022, by Netflix, "Troll" garnered positive reviews for its performances, score, cinematography, and visual effects, despite some criticism for its derivative plot. The film's success has prompted the development of a sequel. The plot intricately weaves together elements of fantasy, science fiction, and Norwegian folklore, making "Troll" an engaging cinematic experience that captivates audiences with its action sequences and mythical undertones.
