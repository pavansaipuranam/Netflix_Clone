About Sweet Tooth:

"Sweet Tooth" is an American fantasy drama television series developed by Jim Mickle, based on the comic book series created by Jeff Lemire and published by DC Comics' Vertigo imprint. Premiering on Netflix in June 2021, the show's second season was released in April 2023, and a third and final season was announced the following month. The series is set in a world where a viral pandemic known as the Sick (or H5G9 virus) has devastated the human population. Concurrently, hybrid babies are born with animal characteristics, leading to societal collapse. Many humans, uncertain whether hybrids caused the virus or resulted from it, fear and hunt them. The story follows Gus, a naive 10-year-old part-deer hybrid, who embarks on a journey to find his mother after his father's death.


Gus, the main character, accidentally starts a fire that attracts hunters. Tommy Jepperd, a lone traveler drawn by the fire, saves Gus. Despite Jepperd's initial reluctance, Gus convinces him to escort him to Colorado to find his mother. The narrative also explores Dr. Aditya Singh's quest to cure the Sick, Bear's leadership of the Animal Army protecting hybrids, and the sanctuary called "The Preserve," run by Aimee Eden.
