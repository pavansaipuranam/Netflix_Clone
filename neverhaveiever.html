About Never Have I Ever:

"Never Have I Ever" is an American comedy-drama television series that follows the story of an Indian-American high school student navigating the challenges of adolescence after the sudden death of her father. Created by Mindy Kaling and Lang Fisher, the show premiered on Netflix on April 27, 2020. While the series is set in the San Fernando Valley, it draws inspiration from Kaling's own childhood experiences in the Boston area, capturing the essence of her upbringing.


The show, starring Maitreyi Ramakrishnan in the lead role, has garnered critical acclaim for its portrayal of South Asian representation in Hollywood. It has been recognized as a milestone in breaking Asian stereotypes and providing a fresh perspective on the coming-of-age narrative.The series, through its humor and relatable storytelling, has resonated with audiences, contributing to its positive reception in the world of television.
