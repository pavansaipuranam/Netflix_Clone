About Chor Nikal Ke Bhaga:

In the fictional city of Al-Barkat, Ankit Sethi, the owner of a diamond insurance company, and Neha Grover, a flight attendant, devise a plan to steal diamonds from a government official during a staged hijacking. Ankit owes a significant debt due to the loss of a client's diamonds, and the couple decides to carry out the robbery to settle the debt. However, as the intricate plan unfolds, it is revealed that Ankit's affection for Neha was a facade, and she, along with her accomplice Sudhanshu, orchestrated the entire scheme to seek revenge on Ankit for using her. The elaborate plot involves fake diamonds, an impersonated Flight Marshal, and a cover-up, leading to a complex web of deceit and betrayal.

In a surprising twist, after the staged hijacking and the revelation of the fake diamonds, Neha exposes Ankit's true intentions to the authorities and the client. Ankit, now tortured by those seeking revenge, learns the extent of Neha's retaliation. The story concludes with both characters having extracted their vengeance, leaving the audience in suspense as Ankit hints at his own plans for retaliation against Neha, adding a layer of intrigue to the complex narrative.
