About Ginny Weds Sunny:

"Ginny Weds Sunny" is a 2020 Indian Hindi romantic comedy film directed by Puneet Khanna and produced by Vinod Bachchan. Starring Yami Gautam as Ginny and Vikrant Massey as Sunny, the film follows a plot where outgoing Ginny initially rejects Sunny for an arranged marriage, but Sunny teams up with Ginny's mother to win her love. The cast also includes Ayesha Raza Mishra, Suhail Nayyar, Rajiv Gupta, and others.

In terms of production, Puneet Khanna made his directorial debut with this film, and Navjot Gulati wrote the story while Sumit Arora handled the dialogues. The music for the film was composed by Payal Dev, Gaurav Chatterji, and Jaan Nissar Lone, with Prasad Sashte providing the background score. The movie features songs, including a special appearance by artists like Mika Singh, Neha Kakkar, and Badshah in the song "Sawan Mein Lag Gayi Aag." Distributed by Netflix, "Ginny Weds Sunny" showcases a light-hearted romantic comedy with the streaming platform being its exclusive release.
