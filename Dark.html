About Dark:

"Dark" is a German science fiction thriller television series co-created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese, which aired for three seasons from 2017 to 2020. The narrative unfolds in the fictional town of Winden, Germany, following characters who embark on a quest for truth after a child's mysterious disappearance. The storyline intricately weaves together the lives of four estranged families, exploring a sinister conspiracy involving time travel that spans generations. The series delves into the existential implications of time, human nature, and life, offering a complex and ambitious narrative.


Debuting on Netflix on December 1, 2017, "Dark" marked the streaming service's first German-language original series. The second season was released on June 21, 2019, and the third and final season concluded on June 27, 2020. The show received acclaim for its cast, direction, writing, tone, visuals, musical score, and the intricate complexity of its storyline. In 2021, the BBC recognized "Dark" as the 58th greatest TV series of the 21st century.
