About Kapil Sharma I'M Not Done Yet:

Kapil Sharma, born Kapil Punj on 2 April 1981, is a multifaceted Indian personality known for his contributions as a stand-up comedian, television host, actor, dubbing artist, producer, and singer. Rising to fame after winning The Great Indian Laughter Challenge season 3 in 2007, he has become a prominent figure in the Indian entertainment industry. Sharma's career has been marked by various achievements, including hosting and producing popular television comedy shows such as Comedy Nights with Kapil and Family Time with Kapil.

As of 2023, Kapil Sharma continues to be a significant presence in the entertainment industry, with his show, The Kapil Sharma Show, airing on Sony Entertainment Television and his movie Zwigato released in the same year. Additionally, Netflix announced a show featuring Sharma in November 2023.
