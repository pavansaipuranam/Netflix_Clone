About Queen Maker:

"Queenmaker," the 2023 South Korean political drama series, unfolds a gripping narrative set in the world of politics. Led by a stellar cast including Kim Hee-ae, Moon So-ri, and Ryu Soo-young, the story revolves around Hwang Do-hee, a strategic genius in image-making, who becomes the campaign manager for Oh Kyung-sook, a human rights lawyer known as the 'rhino of justice.' Together, they navigate the challenging landscape of the Seoul mayoral election, with Do-hee leveraging her skills to help Kyung-sook secure victory. The series intricately explores the complexities of political maneuvering, the clash of ideologies, and the personal journeys of its characters in the pursuit of power.


The supporting cast, featuring characters such as Son Young-sim, the influential chairwoman of Eunsung Group, and Baek Jae-min, another candidate for Seoul mayor, adds depth to the narrative. With themes of power, strategy, and the human side of politics, "Queenmaker" on Netflix presents a compelling portrayal of the dynamics involved in a high-stakes political campaign, capturing the challenges and triumphs of those vying for leadership in the bustling city of Seoul.
