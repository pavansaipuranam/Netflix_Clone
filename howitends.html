About How It Ends:

"How It Ends" is a 2018 American action thriller film directed by David M. Rosenthal and written by Brooks McLaren. The movie features Theo James, Forest Whitaker, Grace Dove, Nicole Ari Parker, Kat Graham, and Mark O'Brien. It was released on July 13, 2018, by Netflix and has a runtime of 114 minutes.

The plot revolves around Will and Samantha (Sam), a young couple expecting their first child. Will travels from Seattle to Chicago to seek permission from Sam's father, Tom, to marry her. However, an unexpected seismic event disrupts life on the West Coast, leading to canceled flights and chaos. Unable to fly back, Will and Tom decide to drive to Seattle to find Sam. Along the way, they face various challenges, including encounters with dangerous individuals and navigating a post-apocalyptic landscape. As the journey progresses, they must confront the unknown and survive in a world unraveling due to a mysterious catastrophe. The film received mixed reviews from audiences despite its popularity on Netflix. The cause and nature of the catastrophic event remain unexplained, adding an element of mystery to the narrative.
