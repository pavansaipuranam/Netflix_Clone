About Jaadugar:

"Jaadugar" is a 2022 Indian Hindi-language sports drama film directed by Sameer Saxena, with a screenplay by Biswapati Sarkar. The film is produced under the banner of the newly established production company Posham Pa Pictures. The storyline revolves around a small football-loving town named Neemuch in Madhya Pradesh. The protagonist, a small-time magician named Magic Meenu, portrayed by Jitendra Kumar, lacks athletic skills but must lead his team to victory in order to win a football trophy and marry his love interest. The challenge lies in the fact that his team hasn't won a game in many years.

The film's premise combines elements of sports and drama, as Magic Meenu, despite lacking athletic prowess, takes on the challenge of leading his team to victory in the football tournament. The motivation behind this effort is his desire to marry his love interest. "jaadugar" explores themes of determination, teamwork, and the pursuit of love against the backdrop of a football-enthusiastic town in Madhya Pradesh.
