About Mission Majnu:

"Mission Majnu," set against the backdrop of the Indo-Pakistani War of 1971, follows Amandeep "Aman" Singh IPS, a RAW field operative operating undercover in Pakistan. Falling in love with a blind girl named Nasreen Hussain and getting married, Aman is tasked with uncovering Pakistan's nuclear weapons program. As he gathers crucial information about the nuclear facility in Kahuta, political changes in India create challenges, leading to a tense race against time to prevent an Israeli attack on Quetta. Aman sacrifices his life to ensure Nasreen's escape to Dubai, where she learns of his true identity through a letter.

The film intricately weaves together elements of espionage, love, and sacrifice as Aman undertakes the perilous "Mission Majnu" to safeguard India's interests during a critical period in history. The story captures the complexity of political dynamics and personal sacrifices made by undercover agents, leaving a lasting impact on the characters and the audience.
