About Class:

"Class," an Indian Hindi-language crime drama thriller television series, unfolds its narrative within the confines of Hampton International, a fictional elite high school in Delhi. Adapted by Ashim Ahluwalia from the Spanish series Elite, the show revolves around the intricate relationships between three working-class students and their affluent counterparts in the school. Produced by Bodhi Tree Multimedia and Future East Film, the series, consisting of 8 episodes, premiered on Netflix on 3 February 2023. Following its initial success, Netflix confirmed the renewal of the series for a second season on 6 March 2023.

The plot centers on Dheeraj, Saba, and Balli, three friends from a working-class background whose local school is destroyed in a fire. They are granted scholarships to attend the prestigious Hampton International, with the scholarships sponsored by Ahuja Properties, the entity responsible for the school fire. At Hampton International, the trio initially faces exclusion from their wealthier peers. However, as the school year progresses, the series delves into a compelling exploration of clashes in lifestyles, resentments, envy, and sexual attraction among the diverse student body.
