About Delhi Crime:

"Delhi Crime" is an Indian police procedural crime drama streaming television series created, written, and directed by Richie Mehta. Produced by Golden Karaven, Ivanhoe Productions, Film Karavan, and Poor Man's Productions, the series stars Shefali Shah, Rasika Dugal, Adil Hussain, and Rajesh Tailang. The first season revolves around the aftermath of the 2012 Delhi gang rape, while the second season focuses on the Chaddi Baniyan Gang.

Filming for the first season commenced in Delhi in January 2018, concluding within 62 days. The series meticulously covers the period from the occurrence of the incident to the final arrest of the suspects. "Delhi Crime" gained recognition when its first two episodes premiered at the 2019 Sundance Film Festival in the Indie Episodic category. The entire series was released on Netflix on March 22, 2019, receiving critical acclaim for its performances, writing, and portrayal of the real-life case that served as its inspiration.
