About Elite:

"Elite" (Spanish: "Élite") is a Spanish teen drama series created for Netflix by Carlos Montero and Darío Madrona. The show is set in the fictional elite high school of Las Encinas, focusing on the dynamics between three working-class students who enroll through a scholarship program and their affluent classmates.


The series distinguishes itself in the teen drama genre by addressing progressive issues and offering alternative perspectives to conventional clichés. "Elite" explores diverse sexual themes and employs a unique narrative structure with a flash-forward plot, integrating a mystery element that unfolds across two timelines within each season.On July 19, 2023, it was announced that the show would receive an eighth and final season, with Mina El Hammani set to reprise her role as Nadia Shanaa. "Elite" has maintained its popularity by skillfully blending teen drama with intricate storytelling and societal commentary.
