About Ozark:

"Ozark" is an American crime drama television series created by Bill Dubuque and Mark Williams for Netflix. Produced by MRC Television and Aggregate Films, the series stars Jason Bateman and Laura Linney as Marty and Wendy Byrde, a married couple who relocate their family to the Lake of the Ozarks to continue laundering money for a Mexican drug cartel. Bateman also serves as a director and executive producer.


The first season was released on July 21, 2017, followed by the second season on August 31, 2018, and the third season on March 27, 2020. Each of the first three seasons consists of 10 episodes. In June 2020, Netflix renewed the series for a fourth and final season, consisting of 14 episodes split into two parts. The first part was released on January 21, 2022, and the second on April 29, 2022. "Ozark" received positive reviews from critics, who praised its tone, directing, production values, and performances, particularly those of Bateman, Linney, and Julia Garner. The series accumulated a total of 45 Primetime Emmy Award nominations, including three for Outstanding Drama Series.
